About the Game

Veil of Mortanox combines atmospheric storytelling with precise 2D platforming action. Players navigate hostile environments, face relentless enemies, and engage in sword combat while collecting Lucarii coins to survive.

Key Features

  • Intuitive sword-based combat system with smooth animations
  • Progressive enemy encounters: hostile bats and challenging mini-bosses
  • Vitalis health system with dynamic damage and healing mechanics
  • Lucarii coins collection used for healing and future upgrades
  • Level advancement tied to player performance (mini-boss every 5 bat kills)
  • Persistent save and load system using JSON files
  • Custom sound design and music composed with FL Studio

Name Meanings & Lore

  • Mortanox: Derived from Latin words "Mors" (death) and "Nox" (night), symbolizing a cursed warrior shrouded in darkness.
  • Lucarii: Inspired by the Latin "Lucrum" meaning wealth or profit, representing the currency Mortanox collects.
  • Vitalis: Latin for "life force", representing the player's health and essence in the game.
  • Veil of Mortanox: Represents the curse and mystery surrounding the protagonist's journey.
